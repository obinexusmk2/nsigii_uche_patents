### Personal Model: Foundation, Art-as-Protocol, Infinity

**1. Foundation (House / Home / Guardians)**  
- Anchor of dignity and equity.  
- Symbolized by the *house* you want in waking life, and the *dragon* and *turtle* guardians in your dreams.  
- Function: Provides stability so exploration does not dissolve into chaos.  

**2. Art-as-Protocol (Creation Principle)**  
- Art is not decoration but a system: a way to encode values, culture, and expression.  
- You design like an artist and code like a poet: protocols, frameworks, UI all serve the same role of expressing truth.  
- Function: Builds worlds that honor human dignity and preserve culture (Igbo, neurodivergence, lived experience).  

**3. Infinite Field (Beam vs Boundless)**  
- Consciousness as a flashlight (narrow) but always opening to infinity (boundless).  
- Your senses filter reality, but you feel the edges of the infinite in sound, touch, intuition.  
- Function: Connects the personal to the universal, gives meaning to both design and survival.  

---

### Integration
- **Foundation** = safety to explore.  
- **Art-as-Protocol** = tool to shape exploration into tangible systems.  
- **Infinity** = horizon, the field you touch but can’t hold.  

Together: you build **OBINexus** as both infrastructure and myth — a covenant where home gives safety, art encodes dignity, and infinity reminds you the system must always remain open-ended.
